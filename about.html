Hi! Thanks for stopping by. I’m Mohamed Siddique, a self-taught product designer with 4+ years of experience designing mobile & desktop products. I enjoy doing all the spectrum of the design process and I push pixels with a purpose. I’m currently designing at Flipkart, to improve the payment & fintech experiences for 250+Mn customers.
Previously, I was working at Freshworks, where I was involved in designing lead generation marketing tools for Freshmarketer product. Before that, I was at MaidSafe, where I was involved in crafting applications for the new internet (SAFE Network). While at college, I studied Computer Science engineering, but design found its way into my life.
I lose track of time when I’m sketching in iPad, curled up in a good book, developing utility tools & plugins, playing cricket or practising my Ukulele.
Please don't hesitate to reach out — I'm always open to meeting people and chatting about design, tech and cricket.
