Hi! Thanks for stopping by. I’m Mohamed Siddique, a self-taught product designer with 3+ years of experience in designing Saas products. I’m currently designing at Freshworks, India. I lose track of time when I’m sketching, curled up in a good book, modeling 3D objects playing cricket or learning Ukulele.
