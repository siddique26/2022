Hi! Thanks for stopping by. I’m Mohamed Siddique, a self-taught product designer with 3+ years of experience designing Saas products. I’m currently designing at Freshworks, India. I enjoy doing all the spectrum of design, and I push pixels with a purpose. Curious by nature, I love learning and experimenting with new technologies.
Previously, I was working at MaidSafe, where I was involved in crafting new internet experience (SAFE Network) as well as designing desktop apps & websites. While at college, I studied Computer Science engineering, but design found its way into my life.
I lose track of time when I’m sketching on iPad, curled up in a good book, modelling 3D objects playing cricket or practising the Ukulele.
Please don't hesitate to reach out — I'm always open to meeting people and chatting about design, tech and cricket.
